Cheese Cake

Description

This cake is perfect for any birthday. It's sweet and addictive to eat.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Batter
  • Strawberrys

Directions

  1. Mix the eggs sugar and batter together
  2. Bake for an hour at 350 degrees in oven
  3. Put the strawberrys as toppings
